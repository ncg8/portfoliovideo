MOVBRG BRAND

COMMERCIAL BRAND
Creation and development of the Movbrg iOS Apps brand, in own business format.

After felt a need for new ideas in music composition i thought start coding small programs to help me on that. In no time i was getting good results and a bunch of new crazy music ideas that i never thought possible before.
So, i wanted to share it with people.

But how? Let´s try Apps.
Why? Because they are simple, fast and you can use it everywhere. Ok, let´s do it.

The next step was to compile all the information and code development in three different App products, each one with a unique generation algorithm directed for his own specific music style. One for Classical Music composition, other for Soul-RnB-Disco and the third for Indie Rock. Done. So, let´s sell it and help other people to make music...

iOS Apps that expand music creativity. Slogan "Ideas inside Apps"

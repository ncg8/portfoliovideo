UX FOR COMMERCIAL PRODUCT
UX Design and UI Design study for music composition App CREATIV JAM.
Problem statement, Background, Business Goals, Research, Insights, Concept, Architecture Information, UI Design, Testing.

TITLE
CREATIV JAM
App that uses unconventional music generation algorithms to Expand creativity in R&B-Soul-Disco music composition style.

FUNCTION
UX-UI Design, Research, Information Architecture.

TIMELINE
Execution time between September to November

PROGRAMS
XCode, ILLUSTRATOR, PHOTOSHOP, ADOBE XD

PROBLEM STATEMENT

One of the biggest issues for music composers is the difficulty to break their own creation patterns, get different ideas and developed it into new musical compositions. "The creativity block problem " .

Other aspect is that, different music styles use different construction elements, structures and processes, and does not really exists a music tool solution specific for each music style that solves the problem.


BACKGROUND
Hypothesis of what to solve that problem

A product with music tools that generate unconventional musical ideas to get inspiration easy, fast, anytime, anywhere.
The solution should be multiple products each one focusing a specific music style, for example, one directed for Classical Music, other for R&B-Soul-Disco, other for Rock, and so on.


Complete Pack

The product should be a complete pack of music composition tools, such as, Unique chord progressions, standard chord progressions, songs, melodies, arpeggios, scale modulation, scale library, chord list, metronome and a text field for lyrics.


Content and Funcionality

The content data provided should be accessible for beginners but deep enough to be valuable for professional composers and musicians. The functionality should be intuitive, simple and instantaneous.


BUSINESS GOALS

The value and advantages in terms of immediate creativity that products like this give to music composers and musicians, opens the possibility to transform it into a business.
Advantages of using the App:

Unusual Ideas - Get ideas different then the habitual patterns
Endless Ideas - Get endless ideas based on algorithm generation
Always Available - Get ideas everywhere, every-time you want
Confidence Boost - Friend that always give a suggestion
Simple and Fast - With two clicks generate multiple music ideas and songs
Composition Device - The App become a composition Hardware device


Business Model

The business model can be an Apps Brand, specializes in expanding creativity for music composers and musicians, from beginner to professional level.
It should be communicated by mailing lists, Youtube promotional videos, music magazines adverts and social media marketing campaigns.



RESEARCH
Survey Results

I surveyed 180 music composers and the majority said that have difficulty to break their own music composition patterns and get new ideas

91%- Have the need for some tool that to help generate new music ideas
15% - Try self made consuming time processes to generate new music ideas
79% - Look for tools to generate new music ideas
85% - Willing to buy tools to generate new music ideas
80% - Said that the App will be very useful for their work



Users Interviews

The interviews were made to:

30 music composers and musicians with ages from 18- 60

All actively working in music composition and instrument playing


Questions

What music elements should be included?

What music results are expected from the App?

What is the expected feeling for App visual color, graphics and font?



INSIGHTS
Main Learning

Only 15% of the music composers create self made music ideas generating processes to break their own patterns, because some don´t know how to do it, and others feel it time consuming.

Music composers look for something that can help them on that, but say that it is hard to find a tool that have all the necessary music resources and easy to use.

They are open to buy a tool that give them new ideas.


Composers Quotes

"Composition helper tools are needed.”

“Self made generating ideas processes are tedious, time consuming, i avoid to do it”

"I need a tool that can give an music inspiration wherever i am working"

TESTING

Test Early Concepts.
The test phase revealed three important things:

One
The music scale selected should be the same for all screens, but the generation buttons should be individual for each screen. That way it is possible to use multiple music generation types for the same - key root scale note.

Second
All screens should be available by swipe and not just by click on the open menu. This way the composer can swipe through multiple music generator types and generate each one individually as complement to the others.

Third
The metronome should play in the background for all screens to allow direct composition and improvisation with metronome in real time.
